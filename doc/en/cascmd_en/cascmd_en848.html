25.8.1  Squares in the plane

See Section 26.7.1 for squares in space.

The square command creates squares.

  • square takes two mandatory arguments and two optional arguments.
    • A,B, two points.
    • Optionally, varc, vard, two variable names.
  • square(A,B ⟨,varc,vard ⟩) returns and draws the square ABCD, where the square is traversed counterclockwise.

    If the arguments varc and vard are given, then C and D will be assigned to them.

Examples

square(1-2i,2+i)
square(1-2i,2+i,C,D)
affix(C), affix(D)
     
−1+2 i,−2−i
