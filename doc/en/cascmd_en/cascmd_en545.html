14.3.3  QR decomposition (for TI compatibility)

The QR command finds the QR decomposition of a matrix.

  • QR takes three arguments:
    • A, a square matrix.
    • q and r, two variable names.
  • QR(A,q,r) returns the matrix R from the QR decomposition of A, and assigns the matrices Q and R to the variables q and r.

Example

QR([[3,5],[4,5]],Q,R)
