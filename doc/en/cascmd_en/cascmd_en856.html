12.8.3  Right triangles in the plane: right_triangle

See section 13.7.3 for right triangles in space.

The right_triangle command takes three arguments and an optional fourth. The three mandatory arguments are two points A and B and a real nonzero number k.

right_triangle returns and draws the right triangle ABC, with the right angle at A and with AB = |k|·AC. If k > 0, then AB to AC is counterclockwise; if k < 0 then AB to AC is clockwise.
Input:

right_triangle(i,-i,2)

right_triangle(i,-i,-2)
