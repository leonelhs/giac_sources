19.2.3  Auto-correlation of a signal

The auto correlation of a vector is its cross correlation with itself (see Section 19.2.2). The auto_correlation command computes the auto correlation of a vector.

  • auto_correlation takes v, a complex vector.
  • auto_correlation(v) returns the cross-correlation of v with itself, v⋆ v.

Example

auto_correlation([2,3,4,3,1,4,5,1,3,1])
