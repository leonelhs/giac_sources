9.2.20  Rewriting trigonometric/hyperbolic functions in terms of half tangent/exponentials

The halftan_hyp2exp command rewrites the trigonometric function in terms of tan(x/2) (like halftan, see Section 9.2.19) and rewrites the hyperbolic functions in terms of their definitions using exponentials, namely:
