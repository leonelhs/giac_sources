5.50.3  ℓ∞ matrix norm: maxnorm

The ℓ∞ norm of a matrix A is maxj,k(|aj,k|). The maxnorm command finds the ℓ∞ norm of a matrix. (See also Section 5.42.1.)

  • maxnorm takes one argument:
    A, a matrix.
  • maxnorm(A returns the ℓ∞ norm of A.

Example.

Input:

maxnorm([[1,2],[3,-4]])

Output:

4
