24.6.9  Altitude of a triangle

The altitude command finds an altitude line of a triangle.

  • altitude takes three arguments: a,b,c, three points.
  • altitude(a,b,c) returns and draws the altitude line to the triangle with vertices a,b,c, through a and perpedicular to the segment from b to c.

Example

T:=triangle(1/2,1+i/2,-2/3+i,color=blue); altitude(1/2,1+i/2,-2/3+i)
