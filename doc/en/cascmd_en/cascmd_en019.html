2.4.10  Geo menu

The Geo menu contains commands that are used to work with two- and three-dimensional geometric figures. The first two entries, Geo ▸ New figure 2d (equivalent to Alt+G) and Geo ▸ New figure 3d (equivalent to Alt+H) create levels for two- and three-dimensional figures, respectively. (See Section 17.1.1.) The other menu items are for working with the figures.
