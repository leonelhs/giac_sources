5.6.5  Union of intervals

In Xcas, the union of two intervals is their convex hull. The union operator is a binary infixed operator that finds the union of two intervals.

Examples

i[1,3] union i[2,4]
     
[1.00000000000000..4.00000000000000]           
i[2,4] union i[6,9]
     
[2.00000000000000..9.00000000000000]
