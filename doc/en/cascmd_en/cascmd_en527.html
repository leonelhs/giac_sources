15.1.9  Basis of the intersection of two subspaces

The ibasis command finds a basis for the intersection of two subspaces of ℝn.

  • ibasis takes two arguments: L1 and L2, two lists of vectors generating two subspaces of ℝn.
  • ibasis(L1,L2) returns a list of vectors forming a basis for the intersection of these two subspaces.

Example

ibasis([[1,2]],[[2,4]])
