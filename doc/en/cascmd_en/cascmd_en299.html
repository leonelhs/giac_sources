9.2.18  Rewriting tan(x) in terms of cos(2x) and sin(2x)

The tan2cossin2 command replaces tan(x) by 1−cos(2x)/sin(2x) in an expression.

  • tan2cossin2 takes expr, an expression containing trigonometric functions.
  • tan2cossin2(expr) returns expr with anything of the form tan(x) replaced by 1−cos(2x)/sin(2x).
