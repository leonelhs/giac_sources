To get the parameterization of the curve, you can use the parameq command (see Section 13.13.8).

Examples

  • Input:
    parameq(bezier(1,1+i,2+i,3-i))
    Output:
    ⎛
    ⎝    		1−t⎞
    ⎠    		3+3 t ⎛
    ⎝    		1−t⎞
    ⎠    		2 ⎛
    ⎝    		1+i⎞
    ⎠    		+3 t2 ⎛
    ⎝    		1−t⎞
    ⎠    		⎛
    ⎝    		2+i⎞
    ⎠    		+t3 ⎛
    ⎝    		3−i⎞
    ⎠
  • Input:
    parameq(bezier(point([0,0,0]),point([1,1,0]),point([0,1,1])))
    Output:
    ⎡
    ⎣    		2 t ⎛
    ⎝    		1−t⎞
    ⎠    		,2 t ⎛
    ⎝    		1−t⎞
    ⎠    		+t2,t2⎤
    ⎦
