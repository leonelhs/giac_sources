10.1.25  Converting a series expansion into a polynomial

The convert command (see Section 9.1.10), with the option polynom, converts a series (see Section 12.5.2) into a polynomial. It should be used for operations like drawing the graph of the Taylor series of a function near a point.

  • convert takes two arguments:
    • series, a series.
    • polynom, the option.
  • convert(series,polynom) returns series with the order_size term replaced by 0.
