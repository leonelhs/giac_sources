24.15.12  Checking whether two lines in the plane are perpendicular

See Section 25.11.4 for checking for perpendicularity in three-dimensional geometry.

The is_perpendicular command determines whether or not two lines are perpendicular.

  • is_perpendicular takes two arguments: L1,L2, two lines.
  • is_perpendicular(L1,L2) returns 1 if L1 and L2 are perpendicular and otherwise returns 0.

Examples

is_perpendicular(line(0,1+i),line(i,1))
     
1           
is_parallel(line(0,1+i),line(1+i,1))
     
0
