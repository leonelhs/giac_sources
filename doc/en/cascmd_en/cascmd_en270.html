8.3.5  Right side of an equation

(See Section 4.2.4, Section 7.2.3, Section 5.5.1, Section 5.6.2, Section 5.3.6, Section 8.3.4, Section 26.2.8 and Section 26.2.9 for other uses of right.)

The right or rhs command finds the right hand side of an equation.

  • right takes lhs=rhs, an equation.
  • right(lhs=rhs) returns rhs.

Example

right(2x-1=3)
     
3
