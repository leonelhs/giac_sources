11.9.14  Factoring over ℤ/pℤ[x]

The factor command factors polynomials with coefficients in ℤ/pℤ. (See also Section 9.1.10.)

  • factor takes P, a polynomial with coefficients in ℤ/pℤ (p must be prime).
  • factor(P) returns P in factored form.

Example

factor((-3*x^3+5*x^2-5*x+4)%13)
