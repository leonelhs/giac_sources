14.1.8  Cross product

The cross or crossP or crossproduct command finds the cross product of two vectors.

  • cross takes two arguments: V1 and V2, two vectors of length 3.
  • cross(V1,V2) returns the cross product of V1 and V2.

Example

cross([1,2,3],[4,3,2])
     
⎡
⎣		−5,10,−5⎤
⎦
          

Indeed, −5=2· 2−3· 3, 10=−1· 2+4· 3, −5=1· 3−2· 4.
