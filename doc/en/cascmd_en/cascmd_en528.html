14.1.8  Basis of a linear subspace

The basis command finds a basis of a linear subspace of ℝn given a spanning set.

  • basis takes L, a list of vectors generating a linear subspace of ℝn.
  • basis(L) returns a list of vectors that is a basis of this linear subspace.

Example

basis([[1,2,3],[1,1,1],[2,3,4]])
