10.7.4  Denominator after simplification

The denom command finds the denominator of a rational function after it has been reduced. (See also Section 6.2.4.)

  • denom takes rat, a rational function.
  • denom(rat) returns the denominator of the irreducible representation of rat.

Examples

denom((x^2-1)/(x-1))
     
1           
denom((x^2+2*x+1)/(x^2-1))
     
x−1
