13.3.2  Addition and subtraction of two matrices

The infixed operator + or .+ (resp. - or .-) is used for matrix addition (resp. subtraction). Note that + and - can also be used as prefixed operators, in which case they must be quoted, like ’+’ and ’-’ (see Section 13.1.3 and Section 13.1.4).
