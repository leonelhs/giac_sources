10.1.8  Rewriting with Heaviside function

The linstep command rewrites piecewise defined expressions, as well as expressions involving step functions (signum and Heaviside) and/or absolute values, as a linear combination of Heaviside functions of linear arguments (see Section 8.3.2).

  • linstep takes one mandatory argument and one optional argument:
    • expr, an expression.
    • Optionally, x, a variable (by default, x=x).
  • linstep(expr ⟨,x ⟩) returns expr written as a sum of terms in form f(x)θ(x−a), where θ denotes Heaviside function.
