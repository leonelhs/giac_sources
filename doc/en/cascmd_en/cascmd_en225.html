2.25.18  Sylvester matrix of two polynomials : sylvester

sylvester takes two polynomials as arguments.
sylvester returns the Sylvester matrix S of these polynomials.
If A(x)=∑i=0i=n aixi and B(x)=∑i=0i=mbixi are 2 polynomials, their Sylvester matrix S is a squared matrix of size m+n where m=degree(B(x)) and n=degree(A(x)). The m first lines are made with the A(x) coefficients, so that :
