6.2.4  Denominator of a fraction after simplification

The denom or getDenom command finds the denominator of a fraction.

  • denom takes r, a fraction.
  • denom(r) returns the denominator of r after it has been reduced to lowest terms. (For rational expressions see Section 10.7.4).

Example

denom(42/12)

or:

getDenom(42/12)
     
2           

To avoid simplification, the argument must be quoted (see Section 8.1.4).

(For rational expressions see Section 10.7.3).

denom('42/12')

or:

getDenom('42/12')
     
12
