14.4.2  ℓ2 matrix norm

The ℓ2 norm of a matrix is an operator norm (see Section 14.4.6) induced by the ℓ2 norm on vectors (see Section 13.1.1). The l2norm or norm command computes the ℓ2 norm of a matrix.

  • l2norm takes A, a matrix.
  • l2norm(A) returns the ℓ2 norm of A.

Example

l2norm([[1,2],[3,-4]])
     
5.11667273602
