25.16.4  Harmonic conjugate

The harmonic_conjugate command finds harmonic conjugates.

  • harmonic_conjugate takes three arguments:
    • P1,P2,P3, three collinear points or three concurrent lines, or three parallel lines.
  • harmonic_conjugate(P1,P2,P3) returns and draws the harmonic conjugate of P1,P2 and P3.

Examples

A:=point(i); B:=point(2+i); C:=point(1/3+i); D:=harmonic_conjugate(A,B,C)
evalc(affix(D))
