20.5.14  Tukey window

The tukey_window command applies the Tukey window function to a sequence or its segment.

  • tukey_window takes one mandatory argument and one to three optional arguments:
    • v, a real vector with length n.
    • Optionally, one of:
      • α, a real number in [0,1] (by default, α=0.5).
      • m,N ⟨,α⟩, a sequence of two integers and optionally a real number α∈[0,1] (by default, m=0 and N=n).
  • tukey_window returns the elementwise product of [vm,vm+1,…,vm+N−1] and the vector w of length N defined by
