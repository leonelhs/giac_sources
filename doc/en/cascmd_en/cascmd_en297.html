9.2.16  Transforming cos(x) into sin(x)/tan(x)

The cos2sintan command replaces cos(x) by sin(x)/tan(x) in an expression.

  • cos2sintan takes expr, an expression containing trigonometric functions.
  • cos2sintan(expr) returns expr with anything of the form cos(x) replaced by sin(x)/tan(x).

Example

cos2sintan(cos(2*x))
