8.3.4  Left side of an equation

(See Section 4.2.4, Section 7.2.3, Section 5.5.1, Section 5.6.2, Section 5.3.6, Section 8.3.5, Section 26.2.8 and Section 26.2.9 for other uses of left.)

The left or lhs command finds the left hand side of an equation.

  • left takes lhs=rhs, an equation.
  • left(lhs=rhs) returns lhs.

Example

left(2x-1=3)
     
2 x−1
