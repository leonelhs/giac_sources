9.1.19  Extracting subexpressions

The part command extracts subexpressions from an expression. (See Section 2.9.2.)

  • part takes two arguments:
    • expr, an expression.
    • n, an integer.
  • part(expr,n) evaluates expr and then returns the nth sub-expression of expr.

Examples

part(x^2+x+1,2)
     
x
