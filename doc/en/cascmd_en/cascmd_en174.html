6.1.10  Euclidean quotient and Euclidean remainder of two integers

The iquorem command finds both the quotient and remainder of two integers (see Section 6.1.8).

  • iquorem takes two arguments: a and b, integers.
  • iquorem(a,b) returns the list [q,r], where q is the quotient and r the remainder of a divided by b.

Examples

iquorem(148,5)

⎣		29,3⎤
