10.9.3  Subtraction in ℤ/pℤ or in ℤ/pℤ[x]

The - operator subtracts two integers in ℤ/pℤ or two polynomials in ℤ/pℤ[x]. (See also Section 6.3.2.) For polynomial expressions, use the normal command to simplify.

Examples

For integers in ℤ/pℤ:

31%13-10%13
     
⎛
⎝		−5⎞
⎠		%13
          

For polynomials with coefficients in ℤ/pℤ:

normal((11*x+5)%13-(8*x+6)%13)

or:

normal(11%13*x+5%13-(8%13*x+6%13))
