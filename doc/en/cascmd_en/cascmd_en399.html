11.7.2  Numerator after simplification

The numer command finds the numerator of a rational function, after it has been reduced. (See also Section 7.2.3.)

  • numer takes rat, a rational function.
  • numer(rat) returns the numerator of the irreducible representation of rat.

Examples

numer((x^2-1)/(x-1))
     
x+1           
numer((x^2+2*x+1)/(x^2-1))
     
x+1
