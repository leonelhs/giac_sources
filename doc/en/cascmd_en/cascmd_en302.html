9.2.13  Transforming complex exponentials into sin and cos

The sincos or exp2trig command uses the identity ei x=cos(x)+isin(x) to rewrite complex exponentials in terms of sine and cosine.

  • sincos takes expr, an expression containing complex exponentials.
  • sincos(expr) rewrites expr in terms of sin and cos.

Examples

sincos(exp(i*x))
     
cosx+i sinx           
exp2trig(exp(-i*x))
