24.10.7  Power of a point relative to a circle

Given a circle C of radius r and a point A at a distance of d from the center of C, the power of A relative to C is d2 − r2.

The powerpc command finds the power of a point relative to a circle.

  • powerpc takes two arguments:
    • C, a circle.
    • P, a point.
  • powerpc(C,P) returns the power of P relative to C.

Example

powerpc(circle(0,1+i),3+i)
     
8
