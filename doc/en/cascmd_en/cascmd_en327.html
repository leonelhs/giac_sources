10.1.9  Polynomial valuation

The valuation of a polynomial is the lowest degree of its non-zero monomials. The valuation or ldegree command finds the valuation of a polynomal.

  • valuation takes P, a polynomial given by a symbolic expression or by the list of its coefficients.
  • valuation(P) returns the valuation of P.

Examples

valuation(x^3+x)
     
1           
valuation([1,0,1,0])
     
1
