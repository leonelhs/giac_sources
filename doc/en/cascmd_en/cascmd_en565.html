14.4.5  Matrix column norm

The column norm of a matrix A is maxj(∑k |aj,k|). (This is also an operator norm; see Section 14.4.6.) The colnorm or colNorm command finds the column norm of a matrix. For matrices, l1norm is also a synonym.

  • colnorm takes A, a matrix
  • colnorm(A) returns the column norm of A.

Example

colnorm([[1,2],[3,-4]])
     
6           

Indeed, max{1+3,2+4}=6.
