3.4.10  The Geo menu

The Geo menu contains commands that are used to work with two- and three-dimensional geometric figures. The first two entries, Geo▸New figure 2d (equivalent to Alt+G) and Geo▸New figure 3d (equivalent to Alt+H) will create a level for creating two- and three-dimensional figures, respecitively. The other menu items are for working with the figures.
