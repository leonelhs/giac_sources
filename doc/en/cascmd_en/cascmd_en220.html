Examples

  • Input:
    limit(1/x,x,0,-1)
    or:
    limit(1/x,x=0,-1)
    Output:
    −∞
  • Input:
    limit(1/x,x,0,1)
    or:
    limit(1/x,x=0,1)
    Output:
    +∞
  • Input:
    limit(1/x,x,0,0)
    or:
    limit(1/x,x,0)
    or:
    limit(1/x,x=0)
    Output:
    ∞
    (Note that ∞ or infinity without an explicit + or - represents unsigned infinity.) Hence, abs(1/x) approaches +∞ when x approaches 0.
